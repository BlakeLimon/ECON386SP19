-This data contains observations from the last year of company X's sales for a particular product line
sold exclusively through their online retail and distribution website.  

-The first observation corresponds to activity on January 1st of the year and observations are recorded at the
close of each daily in sequence.

-Sales are total revenues measured in dollars for the product line in question.

-Price is the products price as listed on the distribution platform (website) on a given day.

-Ad Spending is the amount of social media ad spending measured in dollars and aggregated 
across modes of social media.

-Impressions measure the total social media impressionsfrom the company's and product's social media pages
aggregated across modes of social media.

-Pomotion is a binary variable indicating whether or not the company ran a webside-wide promotion is also recorded 
for each day.
